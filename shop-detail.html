About Decorative Pebbles

Beach and mountain pebbles are also used to create water-smart gardens in areas where water is not abundant. Small pebbles are also used to create living spaces and gardens on the rooftops of buildings.

Indoors, pebbles can be used as bookends and paperweights. Large pebbles are also used to create "pet rocks" for children.
